2024 Bowman Baseball Base / Chrome Parallels

Featuring a refreshed primary design, 2024 Bowman Baseball offers 100 base rookies and veterans along with 150 prospects noting the "1st Bowman" subjects.

As always, these come in paper form, with multiple parallels for each.

2024 Bowman Baseball Inserts

The 2024 Bowman Baseball checklist continues with several inserts joining the rotation. Debuts include Bowman A.I., using advanced stats to predict future MLB stars, and Bowman in Bloom, which shows the young prospects in a flower pot.

Also new, Prospect Power-Up comes with a pinball-themed design.

Among the returning lines are Bowman Scouts' Top 100 (1:4 packs), Bowman Spotlights and ROY Favorites.

Each insert also adds a few parallels.

2024 Bowman Baseball Insert Refractor Parallels Breakdown

  • Mini-Diamond Refractor - #/150
  • Aqua Refractor - #/125
  • Green Refractor - #/99 (Retail only)
  • Gold Refractor - #/50
  • Red Refractor - #/5
  • SuperFractor - 1/1

Fans can also count on another installment of the "ultra-rare" Hidden Gems cards covering rookies and veterans.

2024 Bowman Baseball Autographs

That brings us to the hits and 2024 Bowman Baseball works in a familiar assortment of signed cards.

Chromium collectors have the Chrome Prospect Autograph and Chrome Rookie Autograph cards.

The Paper Prospect Autographs line is exclusive to Retail formats.

From an artistic standpoint , I believe that this year’s Bowman baseball card design is definitely a well thought out and very successful overall design . The borders are not the standard picture frame but are very easy to determine top to bottom and left to right , as far as the centering . The composition of the front of the card is excellent with all the lettering such as the Bowman logo , 1st card , players name , team logo , players position being dispersed evenly in the overall design and not cluttered all in one area , such as the bottom of the card . I am extremely pleased that there are not so many distracting , eye hurting parellels such as the wave design . The next step would be to eliminate the shimmer design altogether . The lunar design is somewhat better as it doesn’t affect the entire pic of the player and only lingers around the edges next to the border . The implementation of the specific color border and the color pattern border along with the green grass border in specific , is definitely a step in the right direction . I am looking forward to the elimination of the speckle , lava , and the remaining gold shimmer . Do you believe that the elimination of these aforementioned eye hurting parellels are a good idea Trey ? I would love to hear your opinion as I value it being that you are a long time collector like myself . I can’t wait to see the card design for the 2024 Topps Series 1 . I realize that you only have what the Topps Company gives you as far as a mock up of the card design . You always seem to get a very well centered version which helps out a lot , by the way . Hope to get a reply from you pertaining to my earlier question . Keep up the good work .

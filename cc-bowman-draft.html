The 2024 Bowman Draft Baseball set continues Bowman's tradition of highlighting the latest MLB draftees and top prospects, offering collectors a comprehensive array of cards that blend base sets, parallels, variations, and autographs. This release is particularly anticipated for its inclusion of numerous "1st Bowman" cards, marking the official Bowman debut for many emerging talents.

Release Date: December 4, 2024

Configuration:

  • Hobby Jumbo Box: 12 packs per box, 32 cards per pack, with three autographs per box.
  • Super Jumbo Box: 5 packs per box, 120 cards per pack, featuring five autographs per box.

Base Set: The base set comprises 200 cards, featuring a mix of the latest MLB draftees and top prospects. Each card is available in both paper and Chrome versions, providing collectors with diverse options.

Parallels: Bowman Draft is renowned for its extensive parallel lineup, offering collectors various levels of rarity and unique designs.

  • Paper Parallels:

    • Blue: Numbered to 150
    • Green: Numbered to 99
    • Gold: Numbered to 50
    • Orange: Numbered to 25
    • Red: Numbered to 5
    • Black: 1/1
    • Printing Plates: 1/1 (four versions per card)
  • Chrome Parallels:
    • Refractor: Unnumbered

    • Purple Refractor: Numbered to 250
    • Sparkles Refractor: Unnumbered

    • Aqua Lunar Crater Refractor: Numbered to 125

    • Steel Metal Refractor: Numbered to 99
    • Yellow Refractor: Numbered to 75

    • Red Refractor: Numbered to 5
    • Red Lava Refractor: Numbered to 5
    • SuperFractor: 1/1
    • Printing Plates: 1/1 (four versions per card)

Variations: To add depth and challenge for collectors, the set includes several special variations:

  • Image Variations: Alternative images for select base cards, offering a fresh visual take on key players.
  • Chrome College Variations: Featuring players in their collegiate uniforms, these variations provide a nod to their amateur careers.
  • Etched in Glass Variations: Designed with a stained-glass aesthetic, these cards offer a unique and artistic representation of 20 select players.
  • Prospector's Special Die-Cut Variations: Die-cut cards limited to 49 copies each, focusing on top prospects with a distinctive design.

Inserts: The 2024 Bowman Draft set includes a variety of insert sets, each with its own theme and design:

  • In Tune: Highlighting players who are in peak form, these cards come with parallels such as Refractor (numbered to 250), Green Refractor (numbered to 99), Gold Refractor (numbered to 50), Red Refractor (numbered to 5), and SuperFractor (1/1).
  • Plasma Power: Showcasing electrifying talents, this set features the same parallel structure as "In Tune," offering collectors multiple levels of rarity.
  • Draft Night: Celebrating the moments players were drafted, these cards also come with Refractor, Green Refractor, Gold Refractor, Red Refractor, and SuperFractor parallels.
  • In Action: Capturing dynamic moments, this set includes Mini-Diamond Refractor (numbered to 150), Green Refractor (numbered to 99), Gold Refractor (numbered to 50), Orange Refractor (numbered to 25), Red Refractor (numbered to 5), and SuperFractor (1/1) parallels.
